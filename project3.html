Fall Detection Using HOG Feature Extraction and Adaptive Boosting Technique

This research aims to develop a reliable fall detection system using accelerometer and gyroscope data, crucial for the safety of the elderly and people with disabilities. It utilizes the Fall Detection Dataset from Kaggle and employs the Histogram of Oriented Gradients (HOG) method for feature extraction. Various classification algorithms like Logistic Regression, K-nearest neighbors (KNN), and decision tree (DT) are explored, alongside ensemble learning AdaBoosting and cross-validation techniques to enhance accuracy. The goal is to overcome existing limitations and contribute to the creation of trustworthy fall detection systems, ensuring timely assistance for vulnerable individuals.
